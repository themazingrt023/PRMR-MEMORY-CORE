## Suitable Invitation Criteria

Invite people who can give useful product feedback while respecting the local controlled-alpha boundary:

- AI startup founders who understand agent memory or operational continuity problems.
- AI agent builders working on long-running workflows, handoffs, or multi-session state.
- SaaS founders with user-history, account-state, or workflow-continuity problems.
- Customer support and operations builders who manage messy customer histories.
- Education AI builders thinking about learner progress continuity.
- Legal or research AI builders handling long case, evidence, or source-history workflows.
- Fintech or risk people who can review synthetic sandbox flows without treating them as real-world validation.
- Technical collaborators who can assess architecture, developer experience, or integration boundaries.
- Accelerator or competition reviewers who need a clear local product walkthrough.

Do not invite someone by implying production access, live API access, certification, or real sensitive-data processing.
