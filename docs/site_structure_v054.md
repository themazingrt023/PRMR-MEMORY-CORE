## Component Notes

HeroSection: brand, product, headline, support line, concept line, and calls to action.

ProblemSection: explain why raw histories are not enough.

SolutionSection: explain continuity packets and review-safe state.

ApiFlowSection: show the backend-mediated flow from event ingest to public-safe report.

DemoPreview: display synthetic replay outputs only.

EvidenceCards: show labelled internal/local controlled-alpha evidence.

UseCaseCards: show AI agent memory, customer support continuity, risk continuity sandbox, and operations knowledge continuity.

AlphaAccessForm: collect controlled alpha interest without handling credentials in the browser.

BoundaryNotice: repeat the evidence boundary wherever claims might be misunderstood.

Footer: include route links, brand, product, and boundary copy.

## Visual System

Background: black and charcoal.

Accents: gold and bronze data streams.

Typography: elegant serif headings and clean technical sans-serif body copy.

Lines: thin borders, grid marks, constellation/network motifs.

Avoid: generic AI robots, crypto aesthetics, exaggerated SaaS claims, and dense marketing hype.
